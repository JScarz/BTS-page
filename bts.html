First-Aid

We offer six different first-aid courses, two of which can be done as shorter refresher sessions.

Fire Safety

The 3 courses we offer are delivered by a former station officer, with over 30 years of experience in the London Fire Brigade.

First-aid Courses:

Paediatrics First-Aid

Length: 3 day full course or 2 day refresher.

Ofsted approved course that covers the roles and responsibilities of an emergency paediatric first-aider. Includes:

  • CPR and Defibrilator use
  • Choking
  • Bleeds, shock, and more
Valid for 3 years.

First-Aid at Work

Length: 3 day full course or 2 day refresher.

This course will instruct you on how to deal with an injured adult in the workplace. Includes:

  • CPR and Defibrilator use
  • Choking
  • Bleeds, shock, and more
Valid for 3 years.

Mental Health First-Aid

Length: 1 day full course or 4 hour half day.

To recognise and respond to the signs and symtoms of mental ill health. Includes:

  • Depression
  • Substance abuse
  • Early signs of mental ill health
  • And more
Valid for 3 years.
